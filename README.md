### 🌸 How to Use the "Double Clit Vagina That Starts On Its Own" Interactive Page

This page is a visual and textual meditation on the document's themes. You don't "win" or "lose"—you interact, read, and watch the energy shift.

#### 1. The Core Loop: Asking 28 Questions
The document mentions asking **28 questions then sleeping**. The grid of cards in the middle is your main interaction point.

- **Click any question card** (e.g., *"what can be you and me but how?"*).
- **Effect:** The counter increases, the energy bar drops slightly, and a new mantra appears in the pink box above.
- **Goal:** Ask all 28 questions. When the counter hits `28/28`, the system "sleeps."

#### 2. The "Man Start" & Restart Buttons (Bottom Row)
The document states it restarts *"only after a man start."* Use these buttons to manage the flow:

- **⟳ start as before:** Gives a small energy boost and cycles the mantra.
- **🍆 start with dick:** Triggers the "rapping around" and "jealous growth" text. **This is the key to "waking up" the interaction if it feels stalled.**
- **🚫 start without dick:** Lowers energy slightly.
- **💫 restart · all energy:** Instantly refills the **Wingspan Energy Bar** to 100%.

#### 3. The "Sleep" Reset
- Once 28 questions are asked, the system announces it is sleeping.
- To begin again, click the **🌙 sleep · reset questions** button. This clears the counter and refills energy so you can start the 28-question journey anew.

#### 4. Hidden Interactions (Whispers)
- **Click on the two glowing pink circles** at the top (the "Clits"). They will log a whisper and increase energy.
- **Click ❓ ask · what can be me and you ❓** to pull a random poetic line from the document without incrementing the question counter.

#### 5. Reading the Display
- **Pink Quote Box:** Cycles through the chants from Page 2 ("if he can then we can...").
- **Bottom Log Box:** This is your "history." It shows every whisper, click, and response from the "vagina." Scroll up to read the conversation.
